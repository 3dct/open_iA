open_iA

A tool for the visual analysis and processing of volumetric datasets, with a focus on industrial computed tomography.

View on GitHub Online User Guide

Stable Release: 2019.07

Includes the Multi-Modal Transfer Functions tool, as presented at the DIR symposium 2019, as well as the FIAKER tool, as presented at the EuroVis conference 2019.
