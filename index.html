open_iA

A tool for the visual analysis and processing of volumetric datasets, with a focus on industrial computed tomography.

Latest Stable Release: 2020.09

Highlights: New AI module, improved filter sampling capabilities.
The Windows binary releases below are available in two versions:
  • Full includes all modules.
  • Lite much smaller, excludes CUDA and modules built on top of it (AI, ASTRA Reconstruction).
